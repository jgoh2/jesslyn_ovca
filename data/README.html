Descriptions of data sets in jesslyn_ovca/data directory

Study: single ell RNA-seq analysis of ovarian cancer ascites Contributer: Itay Tirosh Submission date: Feb 27, 2020 Number of samples (cells): 10906 Platform: Illumina Nextseq 500 BioProject Accession: PRJNA609094 GEO Accession: GSE146026

Overall Design: Ascites samples were either depleted of CD45+ cells and profiled by 10x or sorted by EPCAM+CD24+ and profiled by SmartSeq2.

Files: - GSE146026_Izar_HGSOC_ascites_10x_log.tsv.gz (10x expression matrix) - Izar_2020_10x.RDS (10X Seurat object) - GSE146026_Izar_HGSOC_ascites_SS2_log.tsv.gz (Smartseq2 expression matrix) - Izar_2020_SS2.RDS (SS2 Seurat object)
